Rules During Competition
  1. You may NOT pre-enter any code during practice contest! This is grounds for expulsion
  2. Frivolous submissions and clarifications (especially during the practice contest) are counter to the spirit of the contest and can result in disqualification. Frivolous submissions include, but are not limited to, “testing” the judges on things such as white-space, formatting, etc. YOU HAVE BEEN WARNED!
  3. Items you can and cannot bring into the contest area (which includes any study areas and rest rooms):
    • YES – books *
    • YES - printouts of code *
    • YES - class notes *
    • NO CDs, DVDs, thumb drives or any other USB device
    • NO laptops, NO PDAs, NO cell phones
    • NO headphones/CD Players/MP3 players
    • NO calculators at all. (Use the one on the computer)
      • *You may NOT share these resources!!
  4. During the contest, speak only to the contest staff.
    • If you have a question about a problem statement, use the clarification tool in PC^2.
    • If you have hardware/software issues, speak directly to a staff member.
  5. You may not leave the contest area except to use the restroom. (Escorts will be provided to take contestants to/from the restroom.)
  6. Only the allowed contest languages (C/C++, Python and Java) may be used to solve problems. You are not allowed to utilize system calls to any other languages, such as, but not limited to, perl and python (unless using python of course).
  7. Any attempt to access the Internet (including ftp, email, web or any other remote access) from a contest machine will result in immediate disqualification from the contest.
  8. Any attempt to access another contest machine, either physically or over the network, will result in immediate disqualification from the contest.
  9. Any other behavior, deemed by a contest official to be dishonest, disruptive or otherwise not keeping with the spirit of the contest will also result in a sanction appropriate to the offense.
  10. Do not change any system or environmental paths on your computer - it will likely ruin the contest configuration for your machine.
